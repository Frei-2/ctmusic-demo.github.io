Dataset Description

CTMusic is a Chinese traditional music dataset specifically designed for text-to-music generation. It comprises 741 meticulously annotated text-music pairs with a total duration of 42+ hours, covering a diverse range of traditional Chinese instruments in both solo and ensemble settings.
